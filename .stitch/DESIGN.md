# Design System: CraftCV AI Landing

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
A futuristic, highly dynamic "Cool AI" aesthetic. It should feel deeply technological but accessible. The atmosphere is sleek, utilizing glassmorphism (frosted glass overlays), deep dark space backgrounds, and glowing neon accents. It communicates intelligence, speed, and premium quality.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Void Background** (#09090b) - Used for the primary app background, conveying depth and space.
*   **Neon Indigo Accent** (#6366f1) - Used for primary buttons, active states, and glowing hover effects.
*   **Cyber Purple Accent** (#a855f7) - Used in gradients alongside Indigo for a dynamic "AI" feel.
*   **Frosted Surface** (#ffffff0a) - Used with backdrop-blur for glassmorphic cards and floating sections.
*   **Starlight Text** (#f8fafc) - Used for primary headings and important body copy to ensure high contrast against dark backgrounds.
*   **Muted Stardust Text** (#94a3b8) - Used for secondary text, descriptions, and subtle UI hints.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family:** 'Inter' or a sleek modern sans-serif like 'Outfit'.
*   **Headings:** Bold (800), tight letter spacing, often utilizing gradient text clipping for emphasis.
*   **Body:** Regular (400) weight, relaxed line height for readability.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Pill-shaped (fully rounded), often featuring a subtle background glow or gradient border. Hover states should feel highly reactive (scaling up, increasing glow).
*   **Cards/Containers:** Generously rounded corners (16px - 24px), glassmorphic backgrounds (low opacity white/slate with blur), and a very subtle 1px semi-transparent inner border to define edges against the dark background.
*   **Inputs/Forms:** Dark backgrounds with refined glowing focus rings.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Whitespace:** Generous breathing room (large margins and paddings) to let the glowing elements shine.
*   **Structure:** Single column for mobile, asymmetrical grids or dynamic split planes for desktop hero sections. Floating elements are encouraged.
