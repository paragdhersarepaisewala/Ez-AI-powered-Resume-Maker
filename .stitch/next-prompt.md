A dynamic, highly animated "Cool AI" landing page for an AI-powered Resume Builder, featuring a striking hero section and feature highlights.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark mode, futuristic, glassmorphism
- Background: Deep Void Background (#09090b)
- Primary Accent: Neon Indigo (#6366f1) to Cyber Purple (#a855f7) gradient for buttons and glowing effects
- Surface: Frosted Surface (#ffffff0a) with backdrop blur for cards
- Text Primary: Starlight Text (#f8fafc)
- Text Secondary: Muted Stardust Text (#94a3b8)
- Buttons: Pill-shaped, glowing hover effects, gradient borders
- Cards: Generously rounded (24px), glassmorphic with subtle 1px border

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Sticky navigation bar with the CraftCV logo on the left and a "Build Resume" CTA button on the right. Glassmorphic background.
2. **Hero Section:** Large bold headline ("Craft Your Dream Resume with AI"), subtext, and two buttons ("Get Started Free" and "View Templates"). Include a visually striking, floating 3D-like representation of a glowing digital resume on the right side.
3. **Features Grid:** 3 glassmorphic cards highlighting key features: "AI Text Generation", "ATS Optimized", and "Beautiful Templates". Each card should have a glowing icon.
4. **Social Proof / CTA Section:** A wide banner with a dark gradient, stating "Join 10,000+ professionals" followed by a final, large glowing "Start Building Now" button.
5. **Footer:** Simple links and copyright text in muted stardust text.
